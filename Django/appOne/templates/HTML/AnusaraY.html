Anusara Yoga: The ultimate goal of Anusara yoga practice is to align with the Divine. This is achieved by practicing the 3 A's:
Attitude: Awakening the heart and making it the foundation of your practice, including the desire to align with the Divine.
Alignment: Awareness of the connections between parts of ourselves.
Action: Natural flow of energy, leading to stability and joy.

Anusara emphasizes “Universal Principles of Alignment” as the focal point of its practice, which underlie all of the physical asanas.
